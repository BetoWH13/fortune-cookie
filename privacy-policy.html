Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Misfortune Cookie ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website or purchase one of our custom mugs. We are committed to protecting your privacy while still delivering your daily dose of sarcasm printed on ceramic.

What We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, shipping address, email, and your chosen misfortune quote when you place an order.
  • Payment Data: Payments are processed via PayPal. We don’t store your credit card or PayPal credentials — that’s between you and the payment gods.
  • Technical Info: Like most websites, we collect basic analytics data (like IP address and browser type) via Netlify and optionally cookies, but we keep it chill and anonymous.

How We Use Your Info

  • To process and fulfill your order through Printful (our trusted production partner).
  • To contact you regarding your order status.
  • To improve our website’s performance and user experience.

We Do Not:

  • Sell or rent your data.
  • Send annoying newsletters (we don't even have one).
  • Track your every move across the web — we're not that needy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to make your mug dreams a reality:

  • Printful: For on-demand fulfillment and shipping.
  • PayPal: For secure payment processing.
  • Netlify: For website hosting and forms handling.

Your Rights

You can contact us anytime at hello@misfortunecookie.net to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request we delete your data (unless it’s tied to a paid order already fulfilled)

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally for legal or operational reasons. We'll post any changes here — no shady edits.
